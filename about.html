About Us

AQI24

We are a team in the NASA Space Apps Challenge, working on the Challenge: "From EarthData to Active Cloud Computing with Earth Observation Data for Preaching Cleaves, Safer Sides." Our project uses TEMPO satellite data and cloud computing to monitor and predict air quality, helping communities move toward a cleaner, safer future.

Our Vision

Cleaner Sides for All

We envision a world where every community has access to accurate air quality insights. By combining satellites, we aim to build healthier, safe environments for people everywhere.

From Data to Action

Our vision is to transform earth observation data into meaningful solutions. We come to bridge the gap between our scientific knowledge and everyday decisions that protect our planet.

Breathing the Future

We believe in using innovation to secure clouds for our generation ahead. Through advanced analysis of satellite and ground data, we empower communities to understand, predict, and improve air quality.
